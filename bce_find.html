BCE-Find

Find all accessible websites in one place.

01 Introduction

Bce-Find is a portal where you will find all the accessible content from the internet.

Allows the user find accessible websites for a particular service.

02 Description

BCE-Find brings together all accessible content in one place. You can search by categories and find the perfect website for you, with all accessibility standards thus allowing for perfect navigation on the website. Other internet search engines are unable to find accessible websites. With BCE-Find you will find exactly what you are looking for.
